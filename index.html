About Us

Gold Coast Vibes is a national Afrobeat band formed in 2022 by five young, energetic talents in their early 20s. Inspired by the rich cultural heritage of Ghana and the infectious rhythms of Afrobeat, the band brings fresh, dynamic sounds that resonate with audiences worldwide.
